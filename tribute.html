Stan Lee Bibliography

Stan lee was born in December 28, 1922 in New York, U.S. and died in November 12, 2018, Los Angeles, California. He was a creative and marvelous American Comic Book Writer that is best known for his work with Marvel Comics. Among the hundreds of characters and teams that he helped to create were the “Fantastic Four”, “Spider-Man”, “The Avengers” and “The X-Men”.
